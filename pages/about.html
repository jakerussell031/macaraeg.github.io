A B O U T



Welcome to our NEED MORE SPEED Workshop


where we share our passion for exceptional automobiles with fellow car enthusiasts.


Our collection is the result of decades of dedicated effort, driven by a deep appreciation for the beauty, craftsmanship, and engineering of some of the most iconic cars in history. We have carefully curated a selection of classic and exotic cars that are a true testament to the art of automotive design.


Our team of experts has extensive knowledge of the history and technical details of each car in our collection. We believe that every car tells a story, and we are dedicated to sharing these stories with our visitors, whether they are fellow collectors, enthusiasts, or simply curious visitors.


At our NEED MORE SPEED Workshop, we believe that cars are not just machines, but works of art. That's why we take great care to preserve and showcase each car in its original condition, so that visitors can fully appreciate the beauty and craftsmanship that went into their creation.


Our collection is not just a display of cars, but a celebration of the rich history and culture of the automotive industry. From vintage classics to modern supercars, each car in our collection has a unique story to tell, and we are honored to be the keepers of these stories.


Thank you for visiting our NEED MORE SPEED Workshop. We invite you to explore our collection and share in our passion for exceptional automobiles.
